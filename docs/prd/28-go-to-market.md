# PRD 28 — Go-To-Market

> **Source:** PA 28, lines 11572–11796.

## Area overview

How ConqrAI Wiki is positioned, how it's differentiated against incumbents, and the messaging library that supports those choices.

## Epic 28.1 — Positioning Strategy

### Headline positioning

> **The trusted company brain.** AI-powered, governance-first, enterprise-ready.

### Tagline alternatives

- "Where company knowledge becomes operational intelligence."
- "Wiki + AI + governance. Trusted by your auditor and your engineers."
- "Stop searching. Start knowing."

### Category claim

ConqrAI sits at the intersection of three traditional categories:

- **Wikis** (Confluence, Notion)
- **Knowledge management** (Guru, Tettra)
- **Enterprise search** (Glean)

The category claim is "knowledge governance platform" — not just storage, not just AI search, but the full lifecycle.

## Epic 28.2 — Competitive Differentiation

| Competitor | Their strength | Our edge |
|---|---|---|
| **Confluence** | Maturity, ecosystem | Better editor, AI, governance, simpler permissions |
| **Notion** | Flexibility, modern UX | Permissions, audit, verification, enterprise-grade SSO/SCIM |
| **GitBook** | Polish for public docs | Equal public docs + private wiki + AI in one |
| **Guru** | Verification + cards | Full wiki + verification + AI search instead of cards-only |
| **Glean** | Cross-system AI search | Single source-of-truth wiki with built-in AI search |
| **Bloomfire / Stack Overflow Teams** | Q&A focus | Q&A built into wiki + AI Chat instead of standalone Q&A |

## Epic 28.3 — Messaging Library

### For employees
- "Find the answer in seconds, not the right person."
- "Trusted by AI, verified by humans."

### For knowledge managers
- "Stop the documentation rot. The system finds outdated pages so you don't have to."
- "Know what you don't know — knowledge gaps surfaced automatically."

### For technical teams
- "Document like an engineer: code blocks, diagrams, runbooks, ADRs."
- "Your AI agents can read and write your wiki via MCP."

### For admins / IT
- "Pass your audit. Permissions, MFA, SSO, audit logs, retention — all built in."
- "Self-hosted, air-gapped, or cloud — your choice."

### For external clients
- "Publish a docs portal in minutes — same content, different audience."

## Epic 28.4 — Channel Strategy

- **Inbound** — content marketing on documentation governance, AI knowledge management
- **Self-serve** — Community + Business signups via the website
- **Sales-led** — Enterprise customer engagements
- **Partner** — system integrators for regulated industries (aerospace, medical, finance)
